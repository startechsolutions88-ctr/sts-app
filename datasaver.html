Expert Tech Tips
Auto-Update over Wi-Fi

Go to Play Store Settings > Network Preferences and set Auto-update apps to "Over Wi-Fi only".

WhatsApp Media Auto-Download

Disable auto-download for photos and videos in WhatsApp Settings to save massive amounts of data.

Data Compression

Turn on "Data Saver" in your phone's System Settings to block background data for unused apps.
