Battery Health

The 20-80 Rule

Keep your battery between 20% and 80% to maximize its lifespan. Constant 100% charging creates heat that wears down the battery cells.

Hardware Safety

Avoid "Ghost" Chargers

Cheap, non-original chargers can damage your charging port power and internal motherboard circuits. Always use STS-verified cables.

Software Speed

Weekly Refresh

Restart your phone at least once a week. This clears background "ghost" processes and keeps your software system stable.

Memory Management

Media Cleanup

WhatsApp images and videos are the #1 cause of slow phones. Clear your "Sent" media folders monthly to keep your phone fast.
